GNU ELPA - epoch-view

Description: Minor mode to visualize epoch timestamps

Latest: epoch-view-0.0.1.el, 2012-Nov-29, 3.67kB

Home page: http://elpa.gnu.org/packages/epoch-view.html

Browse repository: CGit or Gitweb

Full description

Use like any other minor mode.  You'll see tooltips with dates
instead of Unix epoch times.  This mode turns on font-lock and
leaves it on forever.  You may or may not like that.
